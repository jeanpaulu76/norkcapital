Three Ways to Earn as an Affiliate

Choose the level of involvement that works for you — from a simple introduction to full loan participation.

🤝
The Hand Off — $500

Refer a client who is actively seeking a loan to purchase a real estate investment and receive $500 on the same day the property closes. Simple introduction, fast reward.

📋
The Full Assist — 25%

Refer a client AND actively participate in gathering all the documents necessary to process and fund the loan. Earn 25% of the total origination fees paid to Nork Capital.

🎯

Get a one-time 25% rebate of Nork Capital origination fees from your very own first closing with us. A great way to get started and benefit from the program personally.
