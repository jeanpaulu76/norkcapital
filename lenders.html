Investments Structured Correctly

Savvy Origination — Smart Servicing

Our private money lenders can expect cash flow derived from annual returns in the range of 8%–15%, depending on the borrower and loan purpose. These returns are reliable because you are not faced with finding new investors as loans are paid off — we continue to offer you opportunities to turn your capital so that your money does not become stale from lack of profitable use.

Additionally, each lender capital investment is secured by a priority lien against the real estate and, furthermore, the loan-to-value (LTV) ratio is purposefully kept at 65%–75% to preserve equity — this hedges risk in the event the property is abandoned or must be liquidated.
